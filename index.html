FAQ

Questions we hear often.

Is Sarthya a replacement for therapy? +
No — Sarthya is a supportive companion, not a clinical tool. For serious mental health concerns, we always encourage connecting with our human counselors or a licensed professional.
Is my data private and confidential? +
Absolutely. Your conversations are encrypted and never shared with third parties. Our human counselors also adhere to professional confidentiality ethics.
How are counselors vetted? +
Every counselor is a certified professional with verified credentials, background checks, and at least 3 years of practice experience.
Can I use Sarthya anonymously? +
Yes. You can start conversations without creating an account. Your display name can be anything you're comfortable with.
What languages does Sarthya support? +
Sarthya currently supports English and Hindi, with more Indian languages coming soon including Tamil, Bengali, Marathi, and Kannada.
What if I'm in a crisis or emergency? +
If you're in immediate danger, please contact iCall (9152987821) or Vandrevala Foundation (1860-2662-345). Sarthya will always provide crisis resources if it detects distress.
